About Me

I was first introduced to coding while working on cross functional teams with software engineers during my time as an Mechanical Engineering undergrad at UC San Diego. After graduating from UCSD I worked as a product engineer at a lithium ion battery startup where I managed various marketable product lines, constructing and implementing both design and document changes for these products. Although I learned many skills from my work as a product engineer, I felt a drive to become a more complete engineer.

Over the past few months I’ve been honing my skills in software development through App Academy’s 16-week, 1000+ hour program, developing skills in JavaScript, Ruby on Rails, React, Redux, PostgreSQL, Node.js, MongoDB, AWS and much more. I can now offer the skills of a more hollistic engineer with capabilities in both the mechanical and software side.
